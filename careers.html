FAQ

Your Questions Answered

Everything you need to know about working at Nexus Corp and the hiring process.

Still have questions?

Our talent team is happy to answer anything.

careers@nexuscorp.io
What does the hiring process look like?
Our process typically involves 4 stages: a 30-min recruiter screen, a technical/skills assessment, two panel interviews with your future team, and a final values conversation with a senior leader. The whole process takes 2–3 weeks.
Are all roles truly remote?
The majority of our roles are fully remote. Some client-facing and leadership roles require occasional travel (6–8 trips per year) or prefer candidates near a major office. Each job listing specifies its location requirements.
Do you sponsor work visas?
Yes, we sponsor H-1B, TN, and other work visas for qualified candidates in the United States, as well as work permits in the UK, Canada, Singapore, and Germany.
What equity do employees receive?
All full-time employees receive RSU grants as part of their compensation package. The size of the grant varies by level. Vesting is 4 years with a 1-year cliff, and we offer early exercise options.
How does performance management work?
We do bi-annual performance reviews with 360-degree feedback. Each employee has an Individual Growth Plan (IGP) co-created with their manager. Promotions happen on a rolling basis, not just annually.
